Our Services

Experience a perfect blend of adventure, comfort, and
hospitality with our exceptional services at Pavana Lake Camping.

Book Now

  • Tent

    Immerse yourself in nature's embrace with our cozy tents, providing a serene and comfortable retreat under the starlit sky.

  • Outdoor Games and Activities

    Engage in various recreational activities and outdoor games that encourage team-building and bonding with nature.

  • Sunset and Sunrise Viewing Points

    Identify scenic spots from where campers can enjoy breathtaking views of sunrise and sunset over Pavana Lake.

  • Barbecue

    Enjoy delicious barbecues with our fantastic facilities, allowing you to relish tasty grilled treats amidst the beautiful scenery of Pavana Lake.

  • DJ Night and Party Time

    Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist you with setting up your tents, campfire arrangements, and any other camping needs.

  • Delicious Dining Options

    Indulge in mouthwatering meals prepared with care and served amidst the scenic surroundings. We offer a variety of food options to cater to different tastes and preferences.

  • Bonfire

    Gather around a crackling bonfire and share stories, laughter, and camaraderie with fellow campers for a memorable evening experience.

  • Clean and Hygienic Washrooms

    We provide clean and well-maintained washroom facilities with running water for your convenience.

  • Nearby Sightseeing Assistance

    We can provide information and assistance with planning visits to nearby attractions like Pavana Dam, Tikona Fort, and Lohagad Fort.

  • Parking Facilities

    We offer designated parking areas for your vehicles, ensuring convenience and safety during your camping trip.

  • 24/7 Security

    Your safety is our top priority. Our campsite is equipped with round-the-clock security personnel to ensure a secure environment during your stay.

  • First Aid Kit

    In case of any minor injuries or medical emergencies, we have a first aid kit on-site to provide immediate assistance.
